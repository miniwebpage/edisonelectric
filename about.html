About Us
EDISON ELECTRIC'S reputation grew, making it one of the largest and most dependable switchboard manufacturers nationwide
EDISON ELECTRIC INTEGRATED, INC. has over three (3) decades of experience in the fabrication and assembly of power substation equipment and other electric and electronic engineering systems. Responding to the growing demand of the local market, EDISON ELECTRIC built a fabrication and assembly plant in Baesa, Quezon City, investing in various machineries and equipment to ensure superior quality products.
With the success of its first distributorship venture with MITSUBISHI ELECTRIC CORP., EDISON ELECTRIC ventured into the distribution of other world-renowned brands like LINCOLN ELECTRIC, SUMITOMO, CIRCUTOR, FORTUNE ELECTRIC& MINAMI Manufacturing with products, such as; welding machines and consumables, and cutting equipment; power factor correction capacitors, controllers, digital multi-function meters, earth leakage relays, power transformers and LED lighting.
Every product manufactured at EDISON ELECTRIC goes through a process of meticulous design, precise fabrication and is expertly assembled and finished. Each product also undergoes strict quality inspection and testing. EDISON ELECTRIC keeps abreast on the latest technology through constant improvement like updating of its machinery and continuous training of its sales force and engineers. With our personalized customer service, clients who need assistance in the selection and specification of custom-designed power systems and factory automation need not worry as our highly-trained engineers will be more than happy to attend to all their requirements. We are also an ISO 9001 quality company since year 2002.
Furthermore, our service does not end with delivery of products. We have established very strong after-sales support to help our customer in installation, testing, commissioning & maintenance of their electrical equipment. After all, dependability is being there when you need it most.
